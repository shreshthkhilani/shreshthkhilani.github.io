Meet Shreshth

Shreshth is an aspiring theatre director, scenic designer, writer, and also a website designer. (Wow to hyphenated vocations!)

He likes realism in his theatre and passes on the sugar in his coffee. He can recite the first sonnet of The Golden Gate by Vikram Seth at command. He thinks a lot about mythology and folklore and wants to bring that to his art. (He's been trying to produce a magical realism podcast to this effect; maybe you can help him out?)
