Reviews

Theatre reviews in Philadelphia are often flattering and mostly white. As a person of color, I’ve read reviews by white authors that fail to flag issues about a show that I’d like to know before I watch the show and that fail to make me feel like my concerns about a show are legitimate after watching a show.

Any commitment to bring equity in hiring and the representation of different voices in an artistic community must go hand-in-hand with the representation of voices of color in the artistic criticism of that community. This is a small attempt to add one more PoC voice to Philadelphia’s art criticism.

Of course, while serving as an outlet for the above, I must acknowledge that this is also an attempt to formalize my own personal criticism practice, and hopefully will also serve as the same for other PoC in the city.
